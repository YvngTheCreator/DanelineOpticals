Professional Eye Care with Style

At Daneline Opticals, we believe that clear vision should never compromise on style. Our expert optometrists provide comprehensive eye examinations using state-of-the-art equipment, while our curated collection of designer frames ensures you look as good as you see.

With locations in both Pretoria and Tembisa, we make premium eye care accessible to everyone. We accept all major medical aids and offer flexible payment options including our popular "Get Now, Pay Later" plan.

Our Services

Comprehensive Eye Tests
